Afdhol 86 | Toko Arafah

This online store is made for 2 stores namely Afdhol 86 and also Toko Arafah. These two shops sell groceries or daily necessities, This online store was created by a group of 9 consisting of 4 members, namely Ksatria, Dani, Azril, Rizky Fadillah. Making this online store aims to make it easier consumers. Toko Arafah is on Jalan Gading Junti Asri complex and the shop is managed by a person employee named Mr. Dian. This shop was founded around 2016. Toko Arafah sells goods groceries or daily necessities. The target of the Toko Arafah owner is to build a house from the sales. Mrs. Yulan as the owner Afdhol 86 shop, the reason for opening an outlet is to find additional income and Yulan's mother chose the name Afdhol 86 because the name was taken from the word good and the best. This shop was established about 2 years ago in December 2019 to be precise. This shop sells groceries in the form of oil, eggs, etc., daily necessities to be precise.
